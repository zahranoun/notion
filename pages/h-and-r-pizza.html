About H&R Pizza And Wings

H&R Pizza and Wings has been proudly serving delicious, high-quality pizzas, chicken wings, and other flavorful foods since 1996. As part of their commitment to enhancing the customer experience, I was given the exciting opportunity to design a rebrand that would help them attract more customers. The app I created is focused on making the ordering process convenient and enjoyable. With its user-friendly interfaces and seamless navigation, the app aims to make the H&R Pizza and Wings experience as comfortable as possible, allowing customers to easily navigate through different sections of the app.
