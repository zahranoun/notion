About amora

Amora is a clean skincare brand dedicated to providing women aged seventeen and older with high-quality organic products tailored to their daily skincare routine. Inspired by Amora's soft, natural, and high-quality aesthetic, the aim is to integrate calming, nurturing, and feminine elements that resonates with women who prioritize self-care and enjoy a touch of fun in their daily skincare rituals. Amora's commitment to natural ingredients and thoughtful design make it the perfect choice for those seeking a delightful and compelling skincare experience.

The project was inspired by the design community on Instagram called Brief Club, a space for designers to create different passion projects.
