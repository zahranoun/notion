Project

Amora is a clean skincare brand dedicated to providing women aged seventeen and older with high-quality organic products tailored to their daily skincare routine. Inspired by Amora's soft, natural, and high-quality aesthetic, the aim is to integrate calming, nurturing, and feminine elements into the brand's visual and interactive design. The focus is on delivering a user-friendly interface and a smooth purchase experience to resonate with women who prioritize self-care and seek a delightful and effective skincare experience.

About amora

With its calming and nurturing pastel colours and appealing illustration, Amora offers an aesthetic that resonates with women who prioritize self-care and enjoy a touch of fun in their daily skincare rituals. Amora's commitment to natural ingredients and thoughtful design make it the perfect choice for those seeking a delightful and compelling skincare experience.
