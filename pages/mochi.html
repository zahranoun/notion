About Oishi Mochi

I was tasked to redesign Magnolia’s e-commerce website and help create their first iOS app. goes here. The new website design increased the conversion rate by 500% and attracted millions of new users and customers. The app has been awarded by Apple several times including as App of the Day. It’s currently rated 4.9/5 in App Store with over 100,000 installs. The app has even been featured by Shopify in official events with Tim Cook and Justin Trudeau (Tim said he loved it!)

The project was inspired by the design community on Instagram called Brief Club, a space for designers to create different passion projects.
