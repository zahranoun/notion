About Boroji Design

At Boroji.com, a small business located in Canada, they bring over 5 years of industry design experience to the table. Boroji's portfolio includes collaborations with large enterprises such as TD Bank of Canada, Statistics Canada, and Public Service Canada. With three academic degrees in Graphic Design, Interactive Media Design, and User Experience Design, their unique blend of design and coding expertise has set them apart. Boroji Design have also shared their knowledge with over 11,000 designers and developers.
